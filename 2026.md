*How to prevent the Indian team from stealing the code.*

*   **‚ùå The Wrong Way:** "Hiding features" or "Security by Obscurity." (Leads to unmaintainable code).
*   **‚úÖ The Right Way: Modular Architecture.**
    *   **Repo A (The Core API):** Contains the "Secret Sauce" (Complex MSEB logic, Algorithms, Pricing). **Only YOU have write access.** Deployed separately.
    *   **Repo B (The Client App):** The Frontend (Next.js/React). The Junior Dev works here. It simply calls your API.
    *   **Result:** If they steal the code, they get a car with no engine.
*   **AI-Assisted Development:**
    *   Write detailed `Spec.md` files.
    *   Let Junior Devs use **Cursor/Claude** to generate boilerplate.
    *   **You are the Gatekeeper:** You review every Pull Request on weekends.
